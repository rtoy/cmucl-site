Download mirrors

The primary CMUCL machine is common-lisp.net, which is accessible by FTP and by HTTP. The machine is located in the United States. We encourage people to use one of the following mirrors, which are updated daily:

ftp.averillpark.net/cmucl
A complete mirror located in the United States.
ftp.linux.org.uk/pub/lisp/cmucl
A complete mirror and primary rsync mirror, located in England.
www.pmsf.de/pub/cmucl
A complete mirror that should provide good bandwidth across Europe and the USA.
ftp.tepus.com:/pub/project/cmucl/
A complete mirror, which should have reasonable bandwidth from Asia.
